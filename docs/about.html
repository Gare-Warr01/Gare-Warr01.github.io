About Me

Garrett Warr

About the Creator

I am Garrett Warr, an undergraduate biotechnology student at Utah Valley University with a strong academic and professional interest in virology, pathology, and public health data science. My work focuses on applying computational tools to real-world biological challenges—particularly those involving infectious disease dynamics and surveillance.

This website was created as the capstone project for my Data Science course. It integrates influenza surveillance data, time-series forecasting models, and interactive dashboards to support public health awareness and predictive analytics.

Scientific Mission

I believe that science should not only inform, but also empower. My core motivation lies in transforming data into actionable insight—telling stories that uncover hidden patterns, assess public health risks, and enable more effective responses to disease outbreaks. I aim to contribute to the fields of epidemiology and translational research, where data meets decision-making.
